Gen Z Nepal Protest

What the Protest is About

Although most people know this as protest about social media ban, it really is not about social media ban. This has been an ongoing issue where the rich kids live luxuriously while the poor struggle to make a living.

Main Points to Know

  1. they have already voted for new prime minister
  2. Started with a few people, but grew to thousands within days
  3. important governers have already fled, while the financial minister has already resigned
  4. Protesters are mainly students but many other adults have joined in
